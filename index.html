About Me

I am a Visiting Researcher in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Illinois Institute of Technology. I earned my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Illinois Tech in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Yu Cheng. My research focuses on information freshness (Age of Information), wireless network optimization, generative AI, machine learning, network security, and blockchain.

In Fall 2024, I served as a Limited-Term Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University Northwest, where I taught Advanced Computer Communication Networks.

Research Interests

  • Age of Information (AoI) in CSMA networks
