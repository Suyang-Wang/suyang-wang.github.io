Biography

I am a Visiting Researcher in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Illinois Institute of Technology. I earned my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Illinois Tech in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Yu Cheng.

I will join the School of Computer Science & Engineering at California State University, San Bernardino (CSUSB) as a tenure-track assistant professor, starting from August, 2025.

My research focuses on information freshness (Age of Information), wireless network optimization, generative AI, machine learning, network security, and blockchain.
